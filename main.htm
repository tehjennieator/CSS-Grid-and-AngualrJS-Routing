Welcome to the Boyce Research Group at FGCU in Fort Myers, Florida! Our group focuses on synthetic organic chemistry and the identification of natural products present in local invasive plant species. Undergraduates interested in joining our team to work on new reaction development, the synthesis of novel therapeutics, or the control of and research on invasive plant species are encouraged to peruse this site then schedule an appointment with Dr. Boyce to discuss what opportunities are available. Also, all students (freshman-senior) are welcome to work with us on our Service Learning and community service endeavors!
